About

I am a software engineer who enjoys making video games for fun. I mainly make games using Rust with the Bevy game engine. My games are among the top popular games tagged with "Bevy" on itch.io and I also won the first place spot of the 2nd Bevy Jam with my game USA Football League Scouting Combine XLV. In my free time I enjoy making music, traveling and spending time with my fiancée and our cat.

Feel free to contact me if you want to collaborate on a game or to tell me you thought my game was trash!
